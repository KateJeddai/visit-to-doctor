Dr. Sai Sudarsan

BBS, MCh - Neuro Surgery, MS - General Surgery, DNB - General Surgery

Neurosurgeon, Spine Surgeon, 33 Years Experience

Dr. Sai Sudarsan is a Neurosurgeon, Spine Surgeon and Neurospine Surgeon in Secunderabad, Hyderabad and has an experience of 33 years in these fields. Dr. Sai Sudarsan practices at Srikara Hospitals in Secunderabad, Hyderabad and Star Hospitals in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad. The doctor completed MBBS from Sri Venkateswara Institute of Medical Sciences (SVIMS), Tirupati in 1986, MCh - Neuro Surgery from Sree Chitra Tirunal Institute for Medical Sciences & Technology, Trivandrum in 1997 and MS - General Surgery from Kurnool Medical College, Kurnool in 1992.

Some of the services provided by the doctor are:

  • Spine Surgery,
  • Pediatric Neurosurgery,
  • Deep Brain Stimulation,
  • Chest Pain Treatment and Peripheral Neuro surgery etc.
